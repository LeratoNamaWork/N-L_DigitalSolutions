FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Our development timeline depends on the package you choose:

  • Basic Website: 5-7 working days after approval
  • Standard Website: 7-10 working days after approval
  • Premium Website: 10-15 working days after approval
  • Mobile Apps: 2-6 weeks depending on complexity

Timelines start after we receive all required content and final approval on designs.

What do I need to provide before we start?

To ensure a smooth process, we recommend having:

  • Company logo and branding materials
  • High-quality images or access to stock photos
  • Written content for your pages
  • Contact information and social media links
  • Examples of websites you like (for design inspiration)
  • List of specific features or functionality you need

Don't worry if you're missing some items - we can help source content and provide guidance!

Do you offer website maintenance and hosting?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance and hosting services:

  • Website Maintenance: R250/month includes updates, security monitoring, and basic content changes
  • Hosting: We can set up hosting for you or guide you through choosing a provider
  • Domain Registration: We'll help you register and manage your domain name
  • Email Setup: Professional business email accounts can be configured

These are additional services billed separately from the website development packages.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Bank transfer (South African banks)
  • Credit/Debit cards via secure payment gateways
  • PayFast
  • SnapScan
  • Payment plans available for larger projects

A 50% deposit is required to start work, with the balance due upon completion. All pricing excludes VAT unless otherwise stated.

Can you help with SEO and getting my website found on Google?

Absolutely! All our websites include basic SEO setup. For more comprehensive SEO, we offer:

  • Basic SEO: Included with all packages - proper structure, meta tags, and mobile optimization
  • Advanced SEO: Available as an add-on - keyword research, content optimization, and technical SEO
  • Ongoing SEO: Monthly SEO management packages available
  • Google My Business: Setup and optimization included with Premium packages

We build websites with search engines in mind from day one!

What happens after my website is live?

Our relationship doesn't end when your website goes live:

  • Training: We provide training on how to update your website
  • Support: 30 days of free support included with all packages
  • Documentation: Complete documentation provided
  • Maintenance Plans: Available for ongoing support and updates
  • Future Updates: We're always available for future enhancements

We consider our clients long-term partners and are always here to help your business grow!
